我发现OneNote某一个分区里面，笔记写多了之后，如果想看看里面有多少比较比较麻烦。
至少我没发现什么直观的办法。
于是写了这个小程序来查看共有多少分区、分区内有多少笔记。
参考：
    http://stackoverflow.com/questions/8186819/creating-new-one-note-2010-page-from-c-sharp
	https://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/magazine/ff796230.aspx
	https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/kb/317597


This is for counting how many notes are there in your OneNote.
As your notes gets more and more, counting how many notes gets harder and harder.
Run this app on your PC, and you will get a tree of all your notes.
